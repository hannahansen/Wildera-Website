FAQ

Common questions

What materials do you use?

Wildera pieces are made to move with you. They are designed to be durable and low maintenance for everyday wear. Each piece will give a brief and accurate description of product materials in their description.

How do I care for my jewelry?

To keep pieces looking their best, store them dry, avoid chemicals like perfume and cleaning products, and remove them before swimming or showering.

Do you offer custom requests?

Unfortunatley not at this time.

Shipping and delivery

Processing time can vary by drop and location. If you need something by a specific date, message me before ordering and I will tell you what is realistic.

Returns and exchanges

If there is an issue with your order, reach out and I will make it right. For hygiene reasons, all products are final sale unless there is a defect.
